About

The countdown component is designed to visualize the countdown to a certain point in time. With Metro UI, you can create countdown simple and easy with role countdown. The countdown is ideal for displaying the time until a certain event, such as a sale or a promotion.

Animation type

You can change an animation type with attribute data-animate and values: fade, slide, zoom, switch (default).
