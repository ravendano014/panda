About

Directive Types

You can use the data-directive attribute to specify the type of directive: note (default), tip, success, info, warning, caution, alert, error, bug, fuck, and important. Use the data-show-icon attribute to show or hide the directive icon. Also, you can change directive title with a data-title attribute.
